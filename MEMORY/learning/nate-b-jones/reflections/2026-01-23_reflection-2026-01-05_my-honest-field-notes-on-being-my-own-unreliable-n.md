## Key Concepts Extracted

**Unreliable Self-Narration**: Humans naturally distort their own narratives through bias, creating an inaccurate internal story that drives decisions without proper scrutiny.
**Governance Through Perspective**: Implementing structured feedback mechanisms (like traditional councils/boards) provides external viewpoints that counterbalance individual blind spots and improve decision-making.
**AI-Democratized Counsel**: Advanced AI makes accessible the kind of advisory perspective that was historically a luxury for leaders, enabling real-time external validation at scale.
**Accountability Architecture**: Building formal systems to question and validate your own thinking prevents unchecked bias from compounding into poor outcomes.
**Perspective as Infrastructure**: Rather than treating feedback as occasional, treating diverse viewpoints as core infrastructure changes how reliably decisions get made.
